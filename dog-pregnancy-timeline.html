Dog Pregnancy Timeline Week by Week

Dog pregnancy typically lasts around 63 days, although a few days of variation can be normal. Understanding the stages of pregnancy can help owners prepare for the arrival of puppies.

Week 1

After mating, fertilization occurs and embryos begin their early development. Most physical changes are not yet visible.

Week 2

The fertilized eggs continue dividing and moving toward the uterus where implantation will occur.

Week 3

Embryos attach to the uterine wall and begin receiving nutrients from the mother. Some dogs may experience mild appetite changes.

Week 4

This is often the stage when a veterinarian may confirm pregnancy through ultrasound examination.

Week 5

Puppies develop rapidly during this stage. The mother's abdomen may begin to enlarge.

Week 6

Weight gain becomes more noticeable. The mother may require additional nutrition and rest.

Week 7

The puppies continue growing and movement may occasionally be observed. Nesting behavior may begin.

Week 8

The mother prepares for delivery. Milk production may start and activity levels often decrease.

Week 9

Most dogs give birth around day 63. Owners should prepare a safe and comfortable whelping area.

Signs Labor Is Near

  • Restlessness
  • Nesting behavior
  • Loss of appetite
  • Drop in body temperature
  • Frequent pacing
