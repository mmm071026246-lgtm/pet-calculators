How to Calculate Dog Age in Human Years

Many people believe that one dog year equals seven human years. However, modern research suggests that dogs age much faster during their first two years of life and then more gradually as adults.

Dog Age Conversion

A one-year-old dog is roughly equivalent to a 15-year-old human. A two-year-old dog is approximately equal to a 24-year-old human. After that, each additional dog year may represent around five human years.

Why Dog Age Matters

Knowing your dog's age in human years can help you understand their health needs, nutritional requirements, and activity level.
