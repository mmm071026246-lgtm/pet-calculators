Dog Pregnancy Calculator

Estimate your dog's due date based on mating date. The average canine pregnancy lasts about 63 days, with a normal range between 58–68 days.




Dog Pregnancy Timeline

  • Weeks 1–2: Fertilization and early development
  • Weeks 3–4: Embryo implantation
  • Weeks 5–6: Rapid fetal growth
  • Weeks 7–8: Physical changes and nesting
  • Week 9: Whelping (birth) may occur

How to Care for a Pregnant Dog

Provide balanced nutrition, avoid stress, and schedule regular veterinary checkups. Watch for signs such as appetite changes, nesting behavior, and temperature drop before labor.

⚠️ This calculator provides an estimate only and should not replace veterinary advice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long are dogs pregnant?

Most dogs are pregnant for about 63 days, with a range of 58–68 days.

Can the due date vary?

Yes, actual delivery may vary based on breed and individual conditions.

When should I contact a vet?

If your dog shows unusual symptoms or passes the expected due date significantly.
