Dog Resting Energy Requirement (RER) Calculator

Calculate your dog's estimated Resting Energy Requirement (RER). RER represents the calories needed to maintain basic body functions while resting.




What Is RER?

Resting Energy Requirement is commonly used by veterinarians as the starting point for estimating daily calorie needs. Activity level, age, reproductive status, and health conditions may increase calorie requirements above the RER value.

RER Formula

RER = 70 × (Body Weight in kg)0.75
