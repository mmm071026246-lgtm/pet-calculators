Estimate your dog's recommended daily food amount based on body weight. Results are estimates and should be adjusted according to activity level, age, and veterinary advice.



Feeding Guidelines

Adult dogs typically require approximately 2% to 3% of their body weight in food per day. Individual needs may vary depending on breed, age, metabolism, and activity level.

Why Proper Feeding Matters

Proper nutrition helps support healthy growth, energy levels, immune function, muscle development, and overall wellbeing. Feeding too little may result in nutritional deficiencies, while overfeeding can contribute to obesity and related health issues.

Factors That Affect Food Requirements

  • Age
  • Breed
  • Body Size
  • Activity Level
  • Reproductive Status
  • Health Conditions

Healthy Weight Management

Maintaining a healthy body weight can help reduce the risk of joint problems, diabetes, heart disease, and other health complications. Regular exercise and appropriate feeding portions are important for long-term health.

Important Note

This calculator provides general estimates only. Individual feeding requirements may vary significantly depending on your dog's lifestyle, metabolism, and overall health. Consult your veterinarian for personalized recommendations.
