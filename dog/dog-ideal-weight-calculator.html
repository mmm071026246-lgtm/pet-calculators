Why Ideal Weight Matters

Maintaining a healthy weight helps reduce the risk of joint problems, heart disease, diabetes, and improves your dog's overall lifespan and quality of life.

How to Check Body Condition

  • ✔ You should feel ribs easily without pressing hard
  • ✔ Visible waist when viewed from above
  • ✔ Tucked abdomen from the side

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my dog is at a healthy weight?

A healthy dog usually has a visible waist and ribs that can be felt easily.

Can breed affect ideal weight?

Yes. Weight varies significantly depending on breed and size.

Is this calculator accurate?

This is an estimate only. Always consult a veterinarian for precise evaluation.
