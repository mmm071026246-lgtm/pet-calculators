How the Dog Size Calculator Works

Puppies grow rapidly during their first year. This calculator estimates adult weight by comparing your puppy's current age and weight to common canine growth patterns.

Size Categories

  • Small: Under 20 lbs
  • Medium: 20–50 lbs
  • Large: 50–90 lbs
  • Giant: Over 90 lbs

Why Predict Adult Dog Size?

Knowing your dog's expected adult size can help with nutrition planning, crate selection, exercise requirements, and veterinary care.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is a dog size calculator?

The calculator provides an estimate based on average growth rates. Genetics and breed differences can affect actual adult size.

When do dogs stop growing?

Small breeds typically stop growing around 12 months, while large and giant breeds may continue growing until 18–24 months.

Can mixed-breed dogs be predicted accurately?

Mixed breeds are harder to predict because growth patterns vary depending on genetic background.

Why does breed matter?

Different breeds grow at different rates and reach different adult sizes.
