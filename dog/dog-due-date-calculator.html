Use this free Dog Due Date Calculator to estimate when your dog may give birth. Most canine pregnancies last approximately 63 days from ovulation.




How Long Are Dogs Pregnant?

Most dog pregnancies last around 63 days, although healthy pregnancies may range from approximately 58 to 68 days depending on timing and individual variation.

Pregnancy Signs in Dogs

  • Reduced appetite during early pregnancy
  • Enlarged nipples
  • Weight gain
  • Increased sleeping
  • Nesting behavior near delivery

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a dog's pregnancy?

Most dogs are pregnant for approximately 63 days.

Is the due date exact?

No. The calculator provides an estimate only. Actual delivery may occur a few days before or after the predicted date.

Should I contact a veterinarian?

Yes. Veterinary care is important throughout pregnancy and before delivery.
