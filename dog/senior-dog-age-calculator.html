Frequently Asked Questions

When is a dog considered a senior?

Most dogs are considered seniors between 7 and 10 years depending on breed and size.

Do large dogs become seniors earlier?

Yes. Large and giant breeds tend to age faster.

Why calculate senior dog age?

It helps optimize care, diet, and exercise.
