Why Dog Aging Is Different

Dogs reach maturity much earlier than humans. The first two years account for most of a dog's developmental growth, while later years progress more gradually.

Life Stages

  • Puppy: 0–1 year
  • Young Adult: 1–3 years
  • Adult: 4–7 years
  • Senior: 8+ years
