Why Exercise Matters

Regular exercise helps maintain a healthy weight, improves cardiovascular health, reduces boredom, and supports mental stimulation.

Exercise Guidelines

  • Small dogs: 30–45 minutes daily
  • Medium dogs: 45–60 minutes daily
  • Large dogs: 60–120 minutes daily

Frequently Asked Questions

How much exercise does a dog need?

Most healthy dogs need between 30 and 120 minutes of exercise daily.

Can exercise help with weight loss?

Yes. Exercise combined with proper nutrition can help overweight dogs lose weight safely.

Do puppies need less exercise?

Yes. Puppies generally need shorter sessions and more rest than adult dogs.
