How Does Dog Weight Loss Work?

Veterinarians generally recommend slow and steady weight loss. Most healthy dogs can safely lose around 1% to 2% of their body weight per week when following an appropriate nutrition and exercise plan.

Common Causes of Weight Gain

  • Overfeeding
  • Too many treats
  • Lack of exercise
  • Age-related metabolism changes
  • Certain medical conditions

Tips for Healthy Weight Loss

  • Measure food portions carefully.
  • Reduce high-calorie treats.
  • Increase daily exercise gradually.
  • Monitor body condition regularly.
  • Consult your veterinarian when needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast should a dog lose weight?

Most dogs should lose weight gradually at a rate of about 1% to 2% of body weight per week.

Can overweight dogs develop health problems?

Yes. Obesity may increase the risk of arthritis, diabetes, heart disease, and reduced lifespan.

Should I consult a veterinarian?

Yes. A veterinarian can help create a safe and effective weight management plan.
