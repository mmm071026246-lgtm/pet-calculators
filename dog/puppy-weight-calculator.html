Puppy Weight Calculator

Estimate your puppy's adult weight based on current age and weight. Results are estimates only and may vary depending on breed, genetics, nutrition, and overall health.





How It Works

This calculator uses a simplified growth estimation formula based on age and current weight. Actual adult size may differ significantly between breeds.

Factors That Affect Adult Weight

  • Breed size
  • Genetics
  • Nutrition
  • Exercise
  • Overall health
Note: Small breeds often reach adult size by 10–12 months, while large breeds may continue growing for up to 24 months.

Understanding Puppy Growth

Puppies grow rapidly during the first year of life. Monitoring weight regularly can help identify whether growth is progressing normally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a puppy's adult weight be predicted?

Yes, but only approximately.

Are puppy weight calculators accurate?

They provide estimates rather than exact predictions.

Do large breeds grow longer than small breeds?

Yes.

When is a dog considered fully grown?

Small breeds ~12 months, large breeds up to 24 months.
