Dog Treat Calorie Calculator

Estimate how many calories from treats your dog can have each day. A common guideline is that treats should not exceed 10% of total daily calorie intake.




Why Limit Treat Calories?

Treats are useful for training and rewards, but too many treats can lead to weight gain and obesity. Keeping treats below 10% of daily calories helps maintain balanced nutrition.
