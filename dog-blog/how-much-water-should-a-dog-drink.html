How Much Water Should a Dog Drink Daily?

Water is essential for a dog's health. Proper hydration supports digestion, circulation, temperature regulation, joint function, and healthy organ performance.

General Water Intake Guidelines

Most healthy dogs require approximately 50 to 60 milliliters of water per kilogram of body weight each day.

Factors That Affect Water Needs

Several factors influence water consumption, including activity level, weather conditions, age, diet, and overall health.

Dry Food vs Wet Food

Dogs eating dry kibble often drink more water because dry food contains very little moisture.

Signs of Dehydration

  • Excessive panting
  • Dry gums
  • Reduced energy levels
  • Loss of appetite
  • Sunken eyes
  • Reduced skin elasticity
Tip: Active dogs often require more water than average.
