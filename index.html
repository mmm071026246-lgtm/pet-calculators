About Pet Calculators

Pet Calculators provides free online tools for pet owners, breeders, animal enthusiasts and veterinary professionals.

Our calculators help estimate pet age, calorie needs, food portions, water intake, pregnancy dates, gestation periods, weight management, aquarium sizing and many other pet care metrics.
